BinaryDiary: A Developer Hub

This was a project I started back in June 2020. It is a developer-focused social platform that allows users to communicate with their peers and showcase their developer credentials.

Users can create profiles (with detailed information on their technical skills, educational and work experience, and the userâ€™s most recent GitHub repositories), view a list of all developers on the platform, and create posts that can be liked/ unliked and commented on by other users.
